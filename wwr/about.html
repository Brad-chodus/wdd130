Whitewater Voyages

A rafting website aims to provide information and facilitate bookings for whitewater rafting experiences. It serves as a platform to educate potential customers about rafting, showcase available trips, and allow for easy online reservations.

History

The development of rafting websites reflects the broader history of whitewater rafting itself, which shifted from early exploration and transport to a recreational activity. Rubber rafts were first designed for military purposes in the mid-19th century, but recreational rafting only gained widespread popularity in the 1970s. Modern rafting websites now provide diverse content, including trip planning resources, safety information, and historical perspectives on the sport.
